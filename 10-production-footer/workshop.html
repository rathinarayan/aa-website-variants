The Workshop

The Data and ICP Workshop. Two hours with our human-led team.

Every engagement starts here. Not with a form. Not with a list. With a two-hour conversation between your team and our strategy team. We extract what actually defines your best customer, translate it into a structured extraction brief, and hand it to our data team to deliver against. No campaign runs until the brief is signed off by you.

Why this matters

Most outbound fails before the first message goes out.

It fails because the list is wrong. Because nobody sat down and worked out what "the right buyer" actually means for this business, this quarter. Because templates got copied and hope got substituted for structure. The Workshop exists so that does not happen to you.

"If the list is wrong, nothing downstream matters. The creative does not save it. The channels do not save it. The AI does not save it. Fix the list, everything else follows."

This is why the Workshop is the first thing we sell, not the last thing we tack on.

The three steps

Two hours, three steps, one signed brief.

The Workshop runs the same way every time. The output is always a structured brief you sign off on before a single campaign touch happens.

1

Discovery (45 minutes)

Our strategist walks through your best customers with you. What patterns do they share? What signals a ready-to-buy buyer? What has and has not worked already? What have the bad-fit clients taught you?

2

Codify (live in the room)

We translate the conversation into a structured ICP brief. Functions, geographies, firm size, must-haves, must-not-haves, priority signals, volume targets. Every field explicit, every assumption surfaced.

3

Approve and extract (next 24 hours)

You sign off on the brief. Our data team extracts the verified list across ASIC, LinkedIn and company records. Our Melbourne-led SDRs start the campaign. Weekly reporting starts the same week.

Common questions

What the Workshop is (and is not).

Who from my team should join the Workshop?

Whoever knows your best customers. Usually the founder or commercial lead. Whoever sold to the last three happy clients. Sometimes the head of customer success has the sharpest instinct. One or two people is ideal; more than four slows the conversation.

Who runs it from your side?

A senior strategist. Narayan often sits in on the first Workshop for a new engagement. The Melbourne account manager who will own the ongoing relationship is always in the room.

Can we do it remotely?

Yes. Most Workshops run on video. In-person is possible in Melbourne by request. No difference in the output.

What if we do not know our ICP yet?

Common. The Workshop is the moment we find it together. We walk backward from your best revenue customers to the pattern that defines them. If the pattern is not there yet, we tell you honestly, and we tell you what else has to be true before outbound is worth running.

How long from Workshop to first meeting booked?

Typical timeline: Workshop day 1. Brief signed day 2. List extracted and verified by end of day 5. Campaigns live day 7-10. First meetings typically week three. Faster if the buyer has a short consideration cycle.

Can we update the brief once campaigns are running?

Yes. Every quarter we do a short refresh Workshop (typically one hour) to adjust inclusion criteria, update signals, and reflect what the campaigns have actually learned. No extra fee.
