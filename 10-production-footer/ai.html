The AI story

Not all AI is the same. Here is how we use it.

A common question we get on every discovery call is "where does AI fit in what you do?" The answer is simpler than the industry makes it sound. Two layers. Nothing in between.

See the two layers What we do not do

In one sentence

"AI lives in two places at AA. Inside our Growth engine, where it multiplies what the SDR team can do. And as a standalone engagement called AI Solutions, where we build AI inside your own operations. That is the whole story."

No third "AI service" bucket. No "AI-as-a-service" vendor lock-in. No pretending AI replaces the human layer. The human SDR team still runs your outbound. AI just lets them reach more of the right people, faster, across more channels than any person could manage alone.

Honesty block

What we do not do.

Equal in importance to what we do. If any of these describes what you want, we are not your partner.

✕

Sell you someone else's SaaS.

We do not resell tools. We do not wrap a markup on someone's subscription. You cover your third-party tool costs directly, you own those credentials.

✕

Replace your team with a chatbot.

AI inside Growth multiplies the human SDR team, it does not replace them. A real person is on every important call. Your account manager is named and based in Melbourne.

✕

Sell AI for AI's sake.

If a problem can be solved faster with a spreadsheet and a human, we say so. AI Solutions is for repetitive, rules-based, or language-heavy tasks. Not for making a chatbot your receptionist.

✕

Build black-box systems you cannot audit.

Everything we build on AI Solutions runs on your credentials. Every decision inside Growth is traceable on your dashboard. Nothing lives in a closed vendor stack we control.

Common questions

When do I need which? And what does it cost?

If I only care about pipeline, do I need AI Solutions?

No. Growth alone handles pipeline. AI inside Growth is bundled with the engagement at no extra line item. AI Solutions is separate and optional. Most clients start with Growth and only add AI Solutions once they trust the work.

Can I do AI Solutions without Growth?

Yes. The two are independent. A restaurant group automating supplier ordering has no connection to lead generation. A law firm automating document review has no connection to lead generation. AI Solutions is sold on its own merits.

How do you price AI inside Growth?

It is bundled. You do not pay a separate line for "AI Outbound". You pay a performance fee when a lead converts, and an all-inclusive monthly where fixed fee makes sense. AI is simply one of the ways the SDR team operates.

How do you price AI Solutions?

Scoped by project. Your exact number in writing within twenty-four hours of the discovery call. You cover any third-party tool costs (OpenAI, cloud hosting, data sources). We carry the build risk.

Is your AI Australian-made?

Our team is. The models we use are the best commercial AI available (mostly US-trained foundation models). Our solution architects tune and deploy them. Data is hosted in your infrastructure or in AU-region cloud where possible, always in line with your compliance rules.

What if AI is not the right answer for my problem?

We will tell you. About one in five discovery calls ends with us saying "AI is not the fix here, you need a process change" or "this is better done by a bookkeeper, not by AI." We would rather send you away happy than sell you something that will not land.
