# Variant 04 · data-forward

The strongest variant for conversion. Built on three deliberate shifts from variants 01, 02, 03.

## Shifts from the earlier variants

1. **Data layer elevated to a hero pillar.** Decision-maker data is AA's strongest technical capability. This variant makes it section 01 of the page, right after the hero, not a sub-point buried inside a service line. "We do not buy databases. We build them." Specific numbers: 42 decision-maker roles, 12 functions, 3 verification sources.

2. **Commercial positioning now genuinely pay-on-results.** The promise is explicit: "Pay only when a lead converts. Not before." Accompanied by a "What you cover" column — the client covers third-party tool costs (inbox warmup, LinkedIn seats, AI voice minutes, CRM seats). Fair skin in the game on both sides. AA carries the outcome risk. Client carries the tooling cost.

3. **Case studies rebuilt as 12 real B2B niches** from AA's actual engagement book (79 verified clients across the Active and Inactive reports). Each niche has a headline, a two-line description of the decision-makers AA targets and the outcomes, and a client count. Business Services and Operations is marked as the featured niche (29 active clients, AA's largest).

## Commercial positioning — exact wording used on the page

**The promise band**
> "Pay only when leads convert · No long contracts, ever · Fair skin-in-the-game on tools · Your price in writing in 24 hours · All ex GST"

**How we charge block (two columns)**
- **What you pay us**: Performance fee only when a lead converts; your team takes the meeting, the deal lands, we get paid. Fixed monthly where it fits. Your exact number in writing in 24 hours. No long contracts, ever.
- **What you cover**: Third-party tool costs (inbox warmup, LinkedIn seats, AI voice minutes, CRM seats). Fair skin in the game. AA carries the outcome risk, you carry the tooling cost. We recommend the stack, you approve it, nothing runs on credentials you cannot audit.

**Closing principle**
> "We align incentives with your business outcome. You invest only in results that hit your pipeline. No setup fees, no retainers, no guesswork."
