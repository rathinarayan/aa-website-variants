# Variant 02 · martell-translated

The bolder read. Borrows the sharpest Martell portfolio moves, translated for Australian B2B.

## Thesis
Builder voice. Near-black hero. Space Grotesk + Geist Mono for a modern, product-feeling layout that still sits inside AA's brand colours. The hero carries a two-line declaration ("Not another tool. An outcome."). AI Solutions adopts Precision's Diagnose → Prescribe → Prove skeleton verbatim as the page spine.

## What it borrows
- "Not another tool. An outcome." (translated from HelloFrank's "Not a dashboard. A dialogue.")
- "We build AI into the business, not onto it." (translated from Alysio's agents-as-first-class-workers)
- "You tell us the job. We build the AI that does it." (translated from Hero Hire's "tell it the role")
- Diagnose → Prescribe → Prove as the Service Line 03 page structure
- Hero numbers block in the Martell press-release style
- Named quote attribution to the pattern, not the company

## What it leaves
- "Hire AI Employees", "Fire your SDRs", "replace your team" — every role-replacement move is softened. "An outbound team you never had to hire" instead of "Fire your outbound team".
- "Digital twin", "autonomous agents on your private server" — product-y framings avoided.
- Waitlist / early access mechanics — AA sells on demand, not hype.
- US aggression — no "24/7", no "$10M+", no "front office".

## Copy moves unique to this variant
- Role-framed headline for each service line ("An outbound team you never had to hire.", "An AI sales layer that fills the calendar.", "AI installed inside the business.")
- Quote wall on the AI page, attributing the pattern honestly
- AI Solutions examples block with four specific industry vignettes (law firm, restaurant, professional services, internal reporting)
- Hero numbers block immediately under the CTA
