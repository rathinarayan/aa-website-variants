03 · Two different AIs

Not all "AI" is the same. Here is how we draw the line.

One of the most common questions we get: "is this AI for my sales team or AI for my business?" Both. They are separate engagements with different teams, different outcomes, different pricing. Never bundled under one vague label.

AI Outbound

Comes with the SDR engagement

  • Who uses it
    Our human SDR team, on your behalf
  • What it does
    Finds buyers, writes messages, runs channels, handles overflow calls
  • Where it lives
    Inside AA's stack. You see the output, not the wiring
  • When you feel it
    More meetings on your calendar, no extra effort
  • How you pay
    Bundled with the SDR engagement. Performance fee per converted lead.

AI Solutions

Separate engagement · different team

  • Who uses it
    Your team. We hand it over and train them.
  • What it does
    Automates internal tasks. Document review, ops, support, reporting.
  • Where it lives
    Inside your business. Your credentials, your data, your control.
  • When you feel it
    Hours back in your team's week. Work that used to wait now moves.
  • How you pay
    Scoped by project. Price in writing within 24 hours.

Both layers share a common idea: AI does the repeatable work so people can do the work that needs people.

06 · The mechanics of the partnership

Pay when it works. Skin in the game on both sides.

The collaboration framed above, costed. We win when you win. The pricing model backs it up.

What you pay us

  • Performance fee only when a lead converts. Your team takes the meeting, the deal lands, we get paid.
  • Fixed monthly where it fits. All-inclusive, ex GST. For work where performance-only is not practical.
  • Your exact number in writing within 24 hours of the discovery call.
  • No long contracts, ever. Month to month.

What you cover

  • Third-party tool costs. Inbox warmup, LinkedIn seats, AI voice minutes, CRM seats.
  • You own the tooling, you own the data trail. Nothing runs on credentials you cannot audit.
  • We recommend the stack. You approve every tool and its cost before it is switched on.
  • Fair skin in the game. We carry outcome risk. You carry tooling cost.
