## Collaboration positioning — exact wording

**Section headline**
> "We are your outbound partner. Not your outbound agency."

**Four partnership principles**
1. **Your wins, our wins.** When your pipeline grows, the performance fee grows with it. When it does not, we do not get paid.
2. **Shared goals, not a statement of work.** The discovery call ends with a shared definition of winning for the next six months.
3. **Skin in the game, on both sides.** We carry the outcome risk. You cover the third-party tool costs.
4. **One Slack, one phone line, one team.** Not a ticket queue. Not an account portal. Founders included in every engagement.

**Closing line**
> "If you win on the back of our work, we win. That is the whole deal."
