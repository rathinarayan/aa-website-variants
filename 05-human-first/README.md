# Variant 05 · human-first

Six shifts from 04, all driven by Narayan's feedback. Does not include the awards strip or video testimonials — those belong to variant 06.

## Six shifts from 04

1. **Home page no longer data-forward.** Hero is result-oriented. The data layer moved down to support the outcome, not lead it.
2. **Human SDR is the foundation.** Section 01 is the team, not data. Three team cards: Melbourne account manager, AA-owned India delivery, founders on every account. SDR engine is the first and largest service line. AI Outbound is an add-on. AI Solutions is a separate engagement.
3. **AI is no longer confusing.** A dedicated "Two different AIs" explainer sits between services and the data layer. Side-by-side table, five rows each: Who uses it, What it does, Where it lives, When you feel it, How you pay.
4. **Scale claim upgraded to "Hundreds of Australian businesses since 2021".** 79+ is the active roster; lifetime is defensibly in the hundreds.
5. **Case studies expanded to 20 B2B niches** (up from 12 in v4). Added SaaS, Legal, Construction, Logistics, Energy, Travel, Media, Events.
6. **Collaboration, not an agency.** New section before the commercial block. Four partnership principles. The commercial block is renamed "the mechanics of the partnership" to reinforce the framing.

## Collaboration positioning — exact wording

**Section headline**
> "We are your outbound partner. Not your outbound agency."

**Four partnership principles**
1. **Your wins, our wins.** When your pipeline grows, the performance fee grows with it. When it does not, we do not get paid.
2. **Shared goals, not a statement of work.** The discovery call ends with a shared definition of winning for the next six months.
3. **Skin in the game, on both sides.** We carry the outcome risk. You cover the third-party tool costs.
4. **One Slack, one phone line, one team.** Not a ticket queue. Not an account portal. Founders included in every engagement.

**Closing line**
> "If you win on the back of our work, we win. That is the whole deal."
