# Variant 11 · simpler-workshop (current front-runner)

v10 with three deliberate changes. Home becomes the full credibility pack. Workshop is simplified and folded back into the Data page. StoryBrand spine reinforced across every page.

## Three changes from v10

### 1. Home page rebuilt as the full credibility pack
People do not click through six tabs on a B2B conversion site. v11 pulls every credibility element into the Home so a buyer can answer every objection without leaving the page:

- Hero + stats strip (carried from v10)
- Promise band (carried)
- **Industries we serve** — eight niches, link to the full twenty on Case Studies
- Two products (Growth engine + AI Solutions) — trimmed feature list
- **Featured case study** — Professional services · Melbourne, navy block with 42 / 6 / 3x
- Why us (three cards)
- **Client video testimonials** — three cards with play buttons
- **Authentic founders' awards strip** — BRW, Deloitte, Ernst & Young, Fast 100
- **Team snippet** — Narayan, Wasim, Melbourne, AA-owned India
- Home video block (carried)
- Finale CTA (carried)

### 2. Workshop simplified and folded back into Data
- `workshop.html` **removed**. The Workshop no longer has its own page.
- Workshop **removed from top nav** (back to six items: Home, Services, AI, Data, Case Studies, Our Team).
- Workshop **removed from footer** columns.
- The Data page has a short `#workshop` anchored section titled "The Data Workshop. Short, simple, sharp." — three cards (Discover, Codify, Extract). No more full ICP card.
- Remaining Workshop references across Home and Services point to `data.html#workshop`.

### 3. StoryBrand on every page
Each page carries a StoryBrand role invisible to the reader but driving the flow.
