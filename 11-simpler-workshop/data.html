How we build data

Four steps. Every campaign.

No short cuts. No shared lists across clients. No buying an enrichment tool and calling it strategy.

1

Define the buyer.

Function before title. Decision rights before hierarchy. Industry, size, region, signals. Your exact ICP, not a template.

2

Extract.

Cross-referenced across ASIC company records, LinkedIn, company websites and specialist data sources. No single-source lists.

3

Verify.

Our team manually checks every record. Current role confirmed. Email and LinkedIn validated. Stale records rebuilt or removed.

4

Refresh.

Continuous refresh as buyer roles shift. Monthly or quarterly depending on the category. Stale data is the enemy of outbound.

Us vs a bought list

The difference you will feel in week one.

A bought list

  • Generic roles from template ICPs
  • Single-source (one platform, one snapshot)
  • 30 to 60 per cent bounce or "moved on" rate
  • Same list sold to hundreds of other agencies
  • Updated once a quarter if you are lucky
  • You find out it is bad when replies stop coming

AA-built data

  • Mapped by function to your specific ICP
  • Cross-verified across ASIC, LinkedIn and company records
  • Under 5 per cent bounce after manual verification
  • Built for you, not reused across clients
  • Refreshed continuously as roles shift
  • You find out it is good when the first meetings land
